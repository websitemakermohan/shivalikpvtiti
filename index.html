Our Courses

Electrician(2 years)

ITI Electrician is a 2-year course, approved by the NCVT (National Council Vocational Training). ITI electrician course is a tradesman specializing in electrical wiring of buildings, transmission lines, stationary machines, and related equipment.

Fitter(2 years)

ITI Fitter is a 2-year course approved by the NCVT (National Council for Vocational Training). The Fitter ITI trade details are related to the mechanical branch. In this course, the candidates will be able to get the knowledge about fittings like Pipe fittings, Machine fitting, and structure fittings.

Plumber(1 year)

ITI plumber is a vocational program which deals with the study of fitting, installing, and maintaining water-related machinery like big water pumps, water generators, water turbines, water pipes, and many other things. The duration of the ITI plumber course is 1 year. A plumber is a trade that comes under the mechanical field.

Basic Cosmetology

Basic Cosmetology is a vocational trade. The full trade consists of one year and its syllabus is divide in two semesters. It is the specific study and application of beauty treatment.
This course is designed to teach the student basic fundamentals of all phases of cosmetology and prepare them to gain entrance into the beginning level job as a qualified cosmetologist.
